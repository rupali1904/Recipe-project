Discription

Japchae (korean Glass Noodles Stir-Fry) is a popular korean dish made with sweet potato glass noodles, vegetables and a savory-sweet soy sauce. Its slightly sweet, flavorful, and often served during celebrations.

Ingredients

For Noodles:

  • 200g sweet potato glass noodles(dangmyeon)

For vegetables:

  • 1 carrot(julienned)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper(sliced)
  • 1 cup sinach
  • 2-3 mushrooms(sliced)

For protein(optional):

  • 100g chicken/beef/tofu

For sauce:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey(optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic(minced)

Instructions

Cook noodles:

  1. Boil noodles for 6-7 minutes
  2. Drain and rinse with cold water
  3. Cut slightly if too long
  4. Prepare vegetables
  5. Heat oil in pan
  6. Stir-fry onion,carrot,and bell pepper
  7. Add mushrooms and cook until soft
  8. Cook spinach
  9. Blanch spinach in hot water for 1 minute
  10. Drain and squeeze excess water
  11. Cook protein (optional)
  12. stir-fry cheken/beef/toufu separately
  13. Mix everything
  14. Add noodles to the pan
  15. Add Sauce ingredients
  16. Mix well on low heat
  17. Finish
  18. Add sipnach and protien
  19. Drizzle sesame oil
  20. Sprinkle sesame seeds

Serving

Serve warm or at roon temprerature ,Perfect as a main dish or side dish

Tips

Adjust sweetness as per taste, You can make it fully vegetarian
